About Us

Insight is our starting point. Impact is our goal.

We are an insight-led ad agency rooted in South Africa, built to help brands win across the African continent.

Born in South Africa. Built for Africa.

Paperjet Wednesday was founded on a conviction that the best advertising starts with understanding people — their lives, their needs, their aspirations. We don't borrow trends from overseas markets. We dig deep into the cultures, behaviours, and dynamics that shape African consumers.

We combine rigorous consumer insight with bold creative thinking to build campaigns that don't just win awards — they move markets, shift perceptions, and drive real commercial results for our clients.
